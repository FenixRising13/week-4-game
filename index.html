The Hardest Button To Button

Your job is to help the soldiers find the buttons they need for their uniforms. I mean, how can they defend your freedom if they can't button their shirt...or pants. Am I right?

Your mission is to gather the buttons to match the number they are requesting.

Each button represents a random quantity, try to pay attention!

Make sure you have the right amount. Too few and we'll lose the battle. Too many and we'll be over budget...again.
